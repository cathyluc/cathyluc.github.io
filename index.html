The Lakes

I want auroras and sad prose
I want to watch wisteria grow right over my bare feet
'Cause I haven't moved in years
And I want you right here
A red rose grew up out of ice frozen ground
With no one around to tweet it
While I bathe in cliffside pools
With my calamitous love and insurmountable grief
- Taylor Swift 'The Lakes'

Guide to the Lakes is a guidebook composed of Romantic poetries about the Lakes District in England written by William Wordsworth. It is frequently studied by scholars as an early influence on the 19th-century geography.
A study from Lancaster University investigated this corpus that comprises 80 works written between 162 and 1900. It demonstrates how a hybrid geographical and corpus-based methodology can be used to study historical writing about the Lakeland region.
